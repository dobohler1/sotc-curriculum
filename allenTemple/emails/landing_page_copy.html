🏀 Basketball IQ Lab is Back — Phase 2 at Allen Temple!

After a successful first phase, we're bringing back the Basketball + STEM Program at Allen Temple Baptist Church for Phase 2. Your child will continue developing their skills on the court while building confidence in math, reading, robotics, and coding in the classroom.

📅 Phase 2 Schedule

April 14 – May 28 (7 weeks)

📍 Allen Temple Baptist Church, 8501 International Blvd, Oakland, CA

🕔 Tuesdays & Thursdays

5:00–6:00 PM – Homework Help & STEM Lab (Math, Reading)

6:00–8:00 PM – Basketball and/or Robotics Practice

💡 Who Can Join

8U & 9U players may participate in official Team California team practices and games (tryout required).

All students up to 12U are welcome for weekly training and classroom sessions — no tryout required!

💥 Program Cost

$25 for the full 7-week session. Phase 1 families have priority registration.

💡 Led by experienced Bay Area coaches with a proven track record of developing athletes. Join a community-focused program designed to help young players succeed on the court, in the classroom, and in life.
