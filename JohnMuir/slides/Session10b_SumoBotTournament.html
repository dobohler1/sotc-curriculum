Final Tuning (10 minutes)

Last chance to adjust before the bracket starts.

  • You may: Change your 3 parameters (speed, search speed, detection distance)
  • You may: Adjust physical modifications (tighten pusher, reposition sensor)
  • You may: Run one practice match against a willing opponent
  • You may NOT: Write brand-new code or change your core strategy
  • You may NOT: Change motor ports or sensor ports

When the timer goes off, hands off the laptops.
